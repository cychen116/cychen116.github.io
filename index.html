About

Chi-Yun Chen is currently a graduate student on a Master of Data Analytics at George Washington University (GWU). This program provides her an opportunity to dig into Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Big Data Management and Analysis and etc. The subject that Chi-Yun is interested in right now is using data science to solve the problem in Agriculture and Food Industry by utilizing the technology to modernize the industry and creating a safer and healthier environment.
